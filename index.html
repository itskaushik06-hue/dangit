02

Three steps.
Zero friction.

01

Select & save as you go

Highlight any text in your AI conversation — a key decision, a constraint, an insight. Right-click → Save to DANGIT. Two seconds. Keep going.

02

Build your session doc

Add your own notes, tasks, and reminders alongside what you saved. Everything lives in one clean doc, organized per conversation.

03

Push smart context back

When the chat drifts, hit Push to Chat. DANGIT reads your doc, generates a smart summary, and injects it straight into the chat. The AI snaps back.

The science behind it

This isn't in your head.
It's architecturally real.

Researchers call it Context Degradation Syndrome — the longer your AI conversation gets, the more the model structurally fails to use information from earlier in the chat. It's not a bug. It's how these models are built.

DANGIT is the workaround. You save the bones of what matters, and we inject it back in a format the AI actually understands — structured, contextual, clear.

>30%
Performance drop once a conversation exceeds the critical context threshold — even when all information is technically still present.
Wang et al., 2026 — Intelligence Degradation in Long-Context LLMs
Lost in middle
LLMs disproportionately prioritize the start and end of conversations. Crucial details in the middle get overlooked by design.
Liu et al., 2023 — Lost in the Middle
