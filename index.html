02 How it works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

01

Select & save

Highlight any text in your AI conversation - a decision, a constraint, an insight. Right-click -> Save to DANGIT. Two seconds. Keep going.

02

Build the doc

Add your own notes, tasks, and reminders alongside saved fragments. One clean document, organized per conversation, per session.

03

Push context back

When the chat drifts, hit Push to Chat. DANGIT reads your session doc and injects a smart, structured context summary. The AI snaps back.

04 The science

This isn't in your head.
It's architecturally real.

Researchers call it Context Degradation Syndrome - the longer your AI conversation grows, the more the model structurally fails to apply information from earlier in the chat. Not a bug. A fundamental architectural limitation.

DANGIT gives the AI a fighting chance. Save what matters, and push it back in a format that's structured, contextual, and actually works.

>30%
Performance drop once a conversation exceeds the critical context threshold - even when all tokens are technically still present in the window.
Wang et al., 2026 - Intelligence Degradation in Long-Context LLMs
Lost in middle
LLMs structurally prioritize the start and end of conversations. Critical details in the middle get systematically deprioritized by design.
Liu et al., 2023 - Lost in the Middle
